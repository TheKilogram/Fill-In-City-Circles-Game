US City Fill — Map Quiz

Goal: Fill the map by naming US cities. Each valid city drops a circle centered on that city. Cities with population ≥ 50k that fall inside any circle appear as small dots. Choose a difficulty to change circle radius.

  • Start typing a city name (e.g., “Denver”) and press Enter.
  • Try to cover as much of the US as possible.
  • Reset clears everything to try again.
